Vibe Vault — LemonSqueezy Product Setup

Click any field to copy, then paste into app.lemonsqueezy.com/products/new. Accent: Graphite Indigo #4F46E5. Solo stays free outside the store.

How to use
  1. Create the product first (name + description).
  2. Add each variant tier (name, price, description).
  3. Paste feature bullets into the variant feature list / custom fields.
  4. Generate images from the AI prompts (Hero, Icon, OG, Thumbnail, then one card per tier).
  5. After checkout variants exist, put the Team (Most Popular) checkout URL into VIBEVAULT_LS_CHECKOUT and landing CTA.
  6. Webhook callback: https://vibevault.lunaos.ai/webhooks/lemonsqueezy (Worker in workers/vibevault).
  7. Wire webhook to issue licenses (Worker secrets + optional Resend), and email the key.
